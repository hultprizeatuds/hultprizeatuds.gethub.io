About Hult Prize at University For Development Studies-Tamale

Welcome to the Hult Prize at the University for Development Studies! We are thrilled that you’re considering being part of a movement that drives social change and creates lasting impact. Joining the Hult Prize is more than just being part of a competition – it’s about taking on the challenge of solving some of the world’s most pressing problems, all while building skills and networks that can propel you into your future.

The Hult Prize Foundation is a global start-up accelerator for social entrepreneurs. It unites the brightest students from around the world to tackle the world’s toughest issues. Recognized by TIME as one of the top five ideas changing the world, the Hult Prize is the largest student competition and crowd-sourcing platform for social good. Every year, the winning team receives $1 million to launch their social enterprise and bring their solution to life.

As a participant in the Hult Prize at UDS, you’ll gain hands-on experience in areas like management, solutions development, and organizational growth. You'll work alongside a diverse group of students from various departments, giving you the opportunity to learn and collaborate across disciplines. Additionally, as part of the world’s largest student competition, you’ll connect with key mentors, industry experts, and like-minded peers, expanding your professional network not only across Africa but globally.

We invite all undergraduate students, from every department, to be a part of this exciting journey. Whether you’re passionate about solving global issues or eager to turn innovative ideas into real-world businesses, the Hult Prize at UDS provides the resources, mentorship, and platform to make your vision a reality. Join us in leading the charge for social change. Together, we can make the world a better place, one solution at a time.
